What Is Sports Massage?

Sports massage is a hands-on treatment that focuses on the muscles, tendons and soft tissues of the body.

It can be used to help with tightness, soreness, muscular tension, restricted movement and recovery after exercise or physical strain.

Your session is tailored to you. That means the treatment will depend on your symptoms, your lifestyle, your activity levels and what you want to get back to doing.

Sports Massage Can Help With

Sports massage may be useful for:

  • Tight hamstrings, calves, quads or glutes
  • Back, neck and shoulder tension
  • Post-workout soreness
  • Running or gym-related tightness
  • Sports injury recovery support
  • Desk posture tension
  • Muscle knots and restricted movement
  • General aches and stiffness

If you are not sure whether sports massage is right for you, you can get in touch before booking.
